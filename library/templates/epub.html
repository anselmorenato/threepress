This page is meant for developers, publishers and ebook enthusiasts who are interested in the adoption of the ePub standard.

About ePub

ePub is the open format of the The International Digital Publishing Forum (IDPF). It is based on XML and XHTML, re-using existing standards for digital books.

The technical details and specification are available from http://openebook.org/.

How Bookworm implements ePub

Because ePub's content format is XHTML, ePub and the web browser are a natural fit. If you use Bookworm, you take advantage of more than 10 years of browser technology.

This allows Bookworm to support advanced layout and markup that standalone reading software cannot, including:

  • Tables
  • Lists
  • Fixed-width fonts

The Table of Contents
